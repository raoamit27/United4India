United for India

A New Architecture of Dissent

As we witness the slow unraveling of India's democratic spirit, we know we can't afford to stay silent. That’s why we need to come together—to create a platform that brings together people of conscience, that helps citizens organize with purpose, and that lays the foundation for a thoughtful, strategic, and lasting movement to renew our civic life.

A National Alliance for Research and Action

Traditional activism has taken us far, but it’s no longer enough. The challenges we face now call for something deeper, more adaptive. That’s why we need a new kind of space—where thoughtful research and lived experience come together, where bold ideas don’t just stay on paper but move people, shift narratives, and drive action. It’s not just a think tank, and it’s not just a movement—it’s both, built for the road ahead.

🧠

The Think Tank

Our intellectual engine. To produce high-quality, evidence-based research, policy briefs, and legal analysis to diagnose the roots of democratic decay and develop credible, constructive alternatives.

💪

The Action Coalition

Our executive arm. To translate ideas into tangible impact through strategic campaigns, grassroots mobilization, and high-impact litigation to defend constitutional values and hold power to account.

How It Will Function: The Three Pillars

Our strategy is built on three interconnected pillars that create a self-reinforcing cycle of network-building, strategic intervention, and protective solidarity.

Weaving the National Fabric

At the core of this initiative will be a nationwide network of professionals—journalists, activists, lawyers, and academics—systematically mapped and connected into a cohesive, pan-Indian coalition.

This network will follow a decentralized hub-and-spoke model, balancing national-level coordination with local-level responsiveness.

A key component will be the Professional Corps—a structured platform that enables unaffiliated doctors, scientists, and tech experts to contribute their skills meaningfully, securely, and at scale.

Strategic Interventions for Impact

Our approach will move beyond reactive responses to proactive influence.

Strategic interventions will include digital advocacy, grassroots mobilization, and targeted Public Interest Litigation (PIL).

Each campaign will be backed by a dedicated policy research unit, ensuring that actions are evidence-based, sharply focused, and designed to drive long-term systemic change—such as electoral reform, institutional accountability, and protection of digital rights.

The Shield of Solidarity

To counter the tactic of isolating and intimidating dissenters, we will establish a robust legal and crisis response system.

A Rapid Response Mechanism will provide immediate support during legal, digital, or on-ground threats.

A Pro Bono Legal Phalanx—a collective of top lawyers and law firms—will be mobilized to offer expert legal defense and protection.
